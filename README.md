Omniflow is a trust-first, AI-powered automation and data intelligence platform
designed to make advanced technology accessible to startups, small businesses,
NGOs, and emerging enterprises.

## Problem
Small organizations struggle with fragmented data, manual workflows,
and lack of transparency. While large enterprises use expensive AI-driven
systems, smaller teams are excluded due to cost and complexity.

## Solution
Omniflow centralizes data, automates workflows, and uses AI to provide
real-time insights, transparency, and accountability through dashboards
and audit logs.

## AI for Good Alignment
- Human-centered AI (decision support, not replacement)
- No black-box decisions
- Transparency-first system design
- Inclusive access to intelligent technology
